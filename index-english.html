Hi! We are MARA.

MARA consists of 20 female voices formed in the autum of 2010. We are a group of young women who have extensive experience of choir singing. Our ambition is high and we prefer creative difficult compositions. Preferably in a modern genre. Always striving to give the audience a holistic experience of choir singing at its best!
